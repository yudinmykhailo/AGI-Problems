# **Problems on the path to AGI**

This repository contains descriptions of fundamental barriers that must be overcome to create Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). The problems are divided into four key groups by their nature.

### **1. Technical problems**

- [Emotions as evaluation](docs/emotions-as-evaluation.md) - using emotional states for rapid task prioritization.
- [Energy efficiency](docs/energy-efficiency.md) - achieving biological levels of energy consumption.
- [Long-term memory](docs/long-term-memory.md) - architectures for accumulating and efficiently retrieving experience.
- [Embodied intelligence](docs/embodied-intelligence.md) - grounding symbols through physical interaction with the world.
- [Robustness](docs/robustness.md) - system resilience in unpredictable conditions.

### **2. Cognitive problems**

- [Meta-learning](docs/meta-learning.md) - the system’s ability to “learn to learn”.
- [Theory of Mind](docs/theory-of-mind.md) - modeling states and intentions of other agents.
- [Causal reasoning](docs/causal-reasoning.md) - moving from correlations to causal models. *(Updated: modern LLMs only mimic causality)*.
- [Compositional generalization](docs/compositional-generalization.md) - building complex concepts from simple ones.
- [Common sense](docs/common-sense.md) - a base of implicit knowledge about the physics and logic of the world.
- [Hierarchical planning](docs/hierarchical-planning.md) - the ability to set goals and plan over vast time horizons.

### **3. Systemic problems**

- [Motivational balance (Alignment)](docs/motivational-balance.md) - balance between “selfishness” and “altruism”, and the problem of inner alignment (mesa-optimization).
- [Autonomous goal setting](docs/goal-setting.md) - transitioning from executing instructions to generating one’s own goals.
- [Recursive self-improvement](docs/recursive-self-improvement.md) - limits and risks of automatic code modification.
- [Social intelligence and cooperation](docs/multi-agent-cooperation.md) - interaction dynamics in environments with multiple independent agents.

### **4. Philosophical problems**

- [Interpretability](docs/interpretability.md) - understanding the internal decision-making processes of the system.
- [Consciousness](docs/consciousness.md) - questions of qualia, subjective experience, and moral status.
