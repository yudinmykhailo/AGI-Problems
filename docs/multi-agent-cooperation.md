# Social intelligence and cooperation

[← Back to list](../README.md)  
**Category:** Systemic / Cognitive  
**Problem status:** Actively researched

---
## The essence of the problem

AGI will act in a world inhabited by other agents (humans and other AIs). It needs to understand not just a single interlocutor (Theory of Mind) but group dynamics, conflicts of interest, and social norms.

---
## Why it is critical

A solitary intelligence, even superhuman, can be socially destructive. Without mechanisms for cooperation and understanding of collective benefit, the emergence of multiple AGIs could lead to catastrophic scenarios of “arms races” or game traps (like the Prisoner’s Dilemma).
